What is Pronote.MG ?

Pronote.MG is a free app that allows you to easily calculate your total average using the subject averages you have on Pronote.

How does it work ?

In the subject bar on the left, enter the name of one of your subjects and its corresponding average in the bar on the right. Repeat this process as many times as needed, then click the "Calculate" button to see your total average. You can always update the average of any subject by modifying its value and then pressing the "Calculate" button again.
